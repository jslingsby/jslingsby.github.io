Drought Monitor for the Jonkershoek Valley, Western Cape, South Africa

This analysis was prepared by the Fynbos Node of the South African Environmental Observation Network (SAEON). Here we present the record of stream flow rates for the Langrivier catchment and rainfall from the Dwarsberg weather station in the Jonkershoek Valley for the period January 1961 to the end of March 2017. We plan to update this page monthly. For live weather data and the record over the past month please access the Dwarsberg weather station directly.

MODIS and Landsat primer for Hannah

This is a quick primer on how to handle MODIS NDVI data from Google Earth Engine (GEE) in R. It’s primarily for my Honours student, Hannah Simon, but why not make it public for all to share? I hope to add Landsat too, but that’ll be another day. For a general introduction for many of the functions used and handling spatial data in general see A Primer for Handling Spatial Data in R posted previously.
